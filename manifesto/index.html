Manifesto for Pearson DevOps


We are uncovering better ways of delivering solutions
by doing it and helping others do it.
Through this work we have come to value:

Optimizing the whole over optimizing by function
 User based measures of service quality over our own perceptions
 Repetition and dedicated practice over special manual processes
 Experimentation, taking risks and continuous learning over controls
 Making more awesome over fighting stupid :)

That is, while there is value in the items on
the right, we value the items on the left more.
